Our Services

What we do


At Honeywest Global Ventures , we deal with the import and export of quality and premium raw cashew nuts , Our job is to collect as many nuts as possible from small and big cashew retailers and also from cashew farmers , then we sundry the nuts and then package them into damage-free Jute Bags in order to be able to export .

Intrested in making us your next supplier and exporter for cashew nuts and even edible products such as Walnuts , Ginger, Sesame Seeds, Cocoa, Soybeans and even locally made Nigerian snacks such as Kuli Kuli (Sugared and salted Groundnut Cake) , Dry Ponmo (Cow Skin) , Garri (Cassava Flakes), Cray Fish and even Cat Fish , Contact us right now.
